# GFS (Global Forecast System)

## What this model is
A global weather forecast model that predicts weather conditions for the entire Earth.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** NOAA / National Weather Service
- **Country / region:** United States

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Global

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Global
- **Typical resolution:** ~28 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to 16 days
- **Update frequency:** 4Ã— daily

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2
- **Official download location:**  
  https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/weather-climate-models/global-forecast

## Notes
This is one of the most widely used global weather models.
