# ARPEGE (Action de Recherche Petite Échelle Grande Échelle)

## What this model is
A global weather forecast model that predicts weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure for the entire Earth.

ARPEGE uses a variable-resolution grid, with higher resolution over Europe and France.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : Météo-France
- **Country / region:** France

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Global (higher resolution over Europe)

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Global
- **Typical resolution:** ~25 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to 4 days (longer ranges available for some parameters)
- **Update frequency:** 4× daily

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2
- **Official download location:**  
  https://meteo.data.gouv.fr/datasets/65bd13e557b26b467363b521

---

## Notes
Météo-France provides free access to selected ARPEGE forecast outputs through its public open data portal.  
The openly available data includes a limited subset of variables compared to the full operational system.
