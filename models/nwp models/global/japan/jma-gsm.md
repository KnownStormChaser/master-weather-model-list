# JMA GSM (Global Spectral Model)

## What this model is
A global weather forecast model that predicts weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure for the entire Earth.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : JMA (Japan Meteorological Agency)
- **Country / region:** Japan

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Global

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Global
- **Typical resolution:** ~20 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to 11 days
- **Update frequency:** 4Ã— daily

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2
- **Official download location:**  
  https://www.wis-jma.go.jp/cms/gsm/download.html

---

## Notes
The Japan Meteorological Agency provides global forecast data openly, though the number of available variables is more limited than some other global models.
