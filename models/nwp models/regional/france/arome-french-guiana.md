# AROME French Guiana

## What this model is
A high-resolution regional weather forecast model designed to predict weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure over French Guiana.

It is optimized for tropical meteorology, including deep convection and heavy rainfall.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : Météo-France
- **Country / region:** France (French Guiana)

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** French Guiana and nearby regions of northern South America

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional (AROME-based)
- **Typical resolution:** ~0.025° (~2.5 km)
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~72 hours
- **Update frequency:** 2–4× daily

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes

This model is tailored for tropical convection and intense rainfall events common in equatorial regions.
