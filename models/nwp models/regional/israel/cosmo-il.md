# COSMO-IL

## What this model is
A high-resolution regional weather forecast model designed to predict weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure over Israel and nearby areas.

It is used for short-range forecasting and performs well for local weather features.

## Who runs it

- **Country / region:** Israel

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Israel and surrounding regions

## Notes
COSMO-IL is based on the COSMO model system, which has been widely used by several European and international weather services.

The model focuses on detailed forecasting over Israel and nearby regions.
