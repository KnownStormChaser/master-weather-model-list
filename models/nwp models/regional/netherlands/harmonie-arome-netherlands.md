# HARMONIE-AROME Netherlands

## What this model is
A high-resolution regional weather forecast model designed to predict small-scale and rapidly changing weather conditions such as showers, thunderstorms, fog, and local wind patterns.

It is optimized for short-range forecasting over the Netherlands and neighboring areas.
