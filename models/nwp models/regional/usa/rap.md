# RAP (Rapid Refresh)

## What this model is
A regional weather forecast model designed for short-range forecasting of rapidly changing weather conditions, such as fronts, wind shifts, temperature changes, and aviation-related weather.

It serves as a bridge between longer-range regional models and very short-range models like HRRR.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : NOAA / National Weather Service
- **Country / region:** United States

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** North America

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional
- **Typical resolution:** ~13 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to 51 hours
- **Update frequency:** Hourly

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2
- **Official download location:**  
  https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/weather-climate-models/rapid-refresh-update

---

## Notes
RAP provides frequent updates and is commonly used for aviation forecasting and short-term weather analysis.  
It shares much of its underlying system with HRRR but runs at lower resolution and longer range.
