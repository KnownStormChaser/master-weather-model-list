# WRF-SMN Argentina

## What this model is
A regional weather forecast model used to predict weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure over Argentina and nearby regions.

It is based on the WRF (Weather Research and Forecasting) model and is adapted for operational forecasting by Argentina’s national weather service.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** Servicio Meteorológico Nacional
- **Country / region:** Argentina

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Argentina and surrounding regions of South America

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional (WRF-based)
- **Typical resolution:** ~7 km (some domains may be higher resolution)
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~72 hours
- **Update frequency:** 2× daily

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2, NetCDF
- **Official download location:**  
  https://registry.opendata.aws/smn-ar-wrf-dataset/

---

## Notes
WRF-SMN is the main regional numerical weather prediction system used operationally in Argentina.

The open data provides selected forecast outputs suitable for public and research use.
