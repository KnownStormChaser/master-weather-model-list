# AROME Antilles

## What this model is
A high-resolution regional weather forecast model designed to predict weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure over the French Antilles.

It is especially useful for forecasting tropical convection, heavy rainfall, and cyclones.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : Météo-France
- **Country / region:** France (French Antilles)

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Caribbean region, including the French Antilles

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional (AROME-based)
- **Typical resolution:** ~0.025° (~2.5 km)
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~72 hours
- **Update frequency:** 2–4× daily

---

## Notes
AROME Antilles plays a key role in short-range forecasting of tropical storms and hurricanes affecting the Caribbean.
