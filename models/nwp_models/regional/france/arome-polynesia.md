# AROME French Polynesia

## What this model is
A high-resolution regional weather forecast model designed to predict weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure over French Polynesia.

It is optimized for forecasting tropical weather across widely dispersed island chains.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : Météo-France
- **Country / region:** France (French Polynesia)

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** French Polynesia and surrounding South Pacific regions

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional (AROME-based)
- **Typical resolution:** ~0.025° (~2.5 km)
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~72 hours
- **Update frequency:** 2–4× daily

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2, NetCDF
- **Official download location:**  
  https://meteo.data.gouv.fr/datasets/65bd1509cc112e6a1458ab95

---

## Notes
This model is particularly valuable for short-range forecasting of tropical showers, trade-wind weather, and cyclone-related impacts in Polynesia.
