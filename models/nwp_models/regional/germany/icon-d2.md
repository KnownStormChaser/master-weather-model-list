# ICON-D2 (ICON Germany – High Resolution)

## What this model is
A high-resolution regional weather forecast model designed to predict small-scale and rapidly changing weather conditions, such as thunderstorms, heavy precipitation, and local wind patterns.

It is the highest-resolution operational weather model run by Germany.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : DWD (Deutscher Wetterdienst)
- **Country / region:** Germany

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Germany and nearby areas

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional
- **Typical resolution:** ~2.2 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to 48 hours
- **Update frequency:** 8× daily

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2
- **Official download location:**  
  https://opendata.dwd.de/weather/nwp/icon-d2/

---

## Notes
ICON-D2 is a convection-allowing model and plays a role similar to HRRR in the United States or HRDPS in Canada.
