# ICON-EU (ICON Europe)

## What this model is
ICON-EU is DWD’s **regional (nested) deterministic** NWP configuration of ICON, focused on Europe.

It is a refined “nest” inside the global ICON system, designed to provide higher-resolution guidance over Europe than the global run.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** Deutscher Wetterdienst (DWD)
- **Country / region:** Germany

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Europe (regional domain)

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional deterministic (nested)
- **Native grid / mesh:** ~6.5 km (R3B08)
- **Domain bounds (approx.):**
  - 23.5°W – 62.5°E
  - 29.5°N – 70.5°N
- **Vertical levels:** 74
- **Model top:** ~22.8 km
- **Update frequency:** multiple cycles per day (DWD provides additional short-range cycles in some suites)

---

## What it provides
- Higher-resolution deterministic guidance for Europe compared to ICON Global:
  - temperature, wind, precipitation, pressure, humidity, clouds
- Lateral boundary conditions / driving data for convection-permitting regional ICON configurations (e.g., ICON-D2 family)

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes (subset via Open Data)
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2
- **Official download location:**  
  https://opendata.dwd.de/weather/nwp/icon-eu/
