# ICON-2I (ICON Italy – High Resolution)

## What this model is
A high-resolution regional weather forecast model designed to predict small-scale and rapidly changing weather conditions such as thunderstorms, heavy precipitation, and local wind patterns over Italy.

It is based on the ICON modeling system and adapted for Italian territory.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : ItaliaMeteo
- **Country / region:** Italy

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Italy and surrounding areas

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional
- **Typical resolution:** ~2 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~48 hours
- **Update frequency:** 4× daily

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2
- **Official download location:**  
  https://dati.agenziaitaliameteo.it/dataset/previsioni-meteorologiche-modello-icon-2i

---

## Notes
ICON-2I is Italy’s primary convection-allowing weather forecast model and plays a role similar to ICON-D2 in Germany or HRDPS in Canada.

The openly available datasets provide a subset of variables compared to internal operational products.
