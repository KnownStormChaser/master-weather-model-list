# HARMONIE-AROME Netherlands (KNMI)

## What this model is
HARMONIE-AROME Netherlands is a **high-resolution regional numerical weather prediction (NWP) model** operated by the Royal Netherlands Meteorological Institute (KNMI).

It is based on the **HARMONIE modeling system (Cycle 43)** and is optimized for short-range forecasting of small-scale and rapidly evolving weather phenomena such as showers, thunderstorms, fog, and local wind patterns over the Netherlands and neighboring regions.

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional deterministic NWP
- **Model system:** HARMONIE-AROME
- **Model version:** Cycle 43 (Cy43)
- **Horizontal resolution:** ~2.0 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to **~60 hours**
- **Temporal output resolution:** 1 hour
- **Run type:** Deterministic (P1)

---

## Model characteristics
- Convection-permitting configuration
- Optimized for:
  - convective precipitation
  - thunderstorms
  - low clouds and fog
  - local wind effects
- Used operationally for warnings and short-range public forecasts in the Netherlands

## Notes
Forecasts are **issued hourly**, but the model itself is not re-run every hour.  
Ensemble versions of HARMONIE are published separately on the KNMI Data Platform.
