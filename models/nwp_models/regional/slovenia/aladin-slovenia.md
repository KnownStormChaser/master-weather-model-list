# ALADIN Slovenia

## What this model is
ALADIN Slovenia is a **regional deterministic numerical weather prediction (NWP) model** operated by the Slovenian Environment Agency (ARSO).

It is part of the **ALADIN limited-area model system (LACE)** and is used for short-range weather forecasting over Slovenia and nearby regions, particularly in complex terrain.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** ARSO (Slovenian Environment Agency)
- **Country / region:** Slovenia

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Slovenia and surrounding regions

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional deterministic NWP
- **Model system:** ALADIN
- **Horizontal resolution:** ~4 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to 72 hours
- **Update frequency:** 4Ã— daily (every 6 hours)

---

## What it provides
Deterministic forecasts of:
- precipitation
- total cloud cover
- surface air pressure
- near-surface temperature, humidity, and wind
- temperature, humidity, and wind at standard pressure levels  
  (925 hPa, 850 hPa, 700 hPa, 500 hPa)

---

## Notes
This entry describes the **ALADIN regional forecast model** (4 km, up to +72 h).

ARSO also provides a **separate high-resolution nowcasting product** at ~1 km resolution with very short lead times (up to +6 h); that is a different system and is not part of the ALADIN forecast model described here.
