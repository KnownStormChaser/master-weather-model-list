# ALARO Belgium

## What this model is
A regional weather forecast model that predicts weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure over Belgium and nearby areas.

It is designed for short-range forecasting and performs well in complex weather situations common in Western Europe.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : Royal Meteorological Institute of Belgium
- **Country / region:** Belgium

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Belgium and surrounding regions

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional
- **Typical resolution:** ~4 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~72 hours
- **Update frequency:** 4× daily

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2
- **Official download location:**  
  https://opendata.meteo.be/ftp/forecasts/alaro_40l/

---

## Notes
ALARO Belgium is part of the ALADIN–HIRLAM model family used by several European national weather services.

The open data provides a selected subset of forecast variables intended for public and research use.
