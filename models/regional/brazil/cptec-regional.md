# CPTEC/INPE Regional Models (5 km & 7 km)

## What this model is
A set of high-resolution regional weather forecast models used to predict weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure over Brazil and surrounding areas.

They are designed for short-range forecasting and provide more local detail than global models.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** CPTEC/INPE
- **Country / region:** Brazil

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Brazil and surrounding regions

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional
- **Typical resolution:**  
  - **~7 km** (larger regional domain)  
  - **~5 km** (higher-resolution domain)
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~72 hours
- **Update frequency:** 2Ã— daily

## Notes
CPTEC provides multiple regional model configurations that differ mainly in resolution and domain size.

These configurations are part of the same operational regional forecasting system and are documented together here.
