# HARMONIE DMI

## What this model is
A regional weather forecast model that predicts weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure over large parts of Europe and the North Atlantic.

It is based on the HARMONIE modeling system and is run operationally by Denmark.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : DMI
- **Country / region:** Denmark

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Large parts of Europe and the North Atlantic, including Iceland

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional (HARMONIE-based)
- **Typical resolution:** ~2 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~48 hours

## Notes
DMI runs its own HARMONIE configuration with a very large domain, extending across Northern Europe and the North Atlantic.

Although similar in structure to other HARMONIE systems, the model is independently operated and distributed by DMI.
