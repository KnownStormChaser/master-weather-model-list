# AROME France

## What this model is
A high-resolution regional weather forecast model designed to predict small-scale and rapidly changing weather conditions such as thunderstorms, heavy precipitation, fog, and local wind patterns.

It is used for short-range forecasting and performs especially well over complex terrain and during convective weather.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : Météo-France
- **Country / region:** France

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** France and nearby areas

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional
- **Typical resolution:** ~1.3 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~48 hours
- **Update frequency:** 4× daily

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2
- **Official download location:**  
  https://meteo.data.gouv.fr/datasets/65bd1247a6238f16e864fa80

---

## Notes
AROME is part of the AROME model family used by several European national weather services.

Météo-France provides open access to selected AROME forecast outputs.  
The openly available data includes a limited subset of variables compared to the full operational system.
