# ICON-EU (ICON Europe)

## What this model is
A regional weather forecast model that predicts weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure across Europe.

It provides higher-resolution forecasts than the global ICON model and is intended for short- to medium-range forecasting.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : DWD (Deutscher Wetterdienst)
- **Country / region:** Germany

## Notes
