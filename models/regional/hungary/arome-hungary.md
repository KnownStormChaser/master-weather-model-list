# AROME Hungary

## What this model is
A high-resolution regional weather forecast model designed to predict small-scale and rapidly changing weather conditions such as thunderstorms, heavy precipitation, and local wind patterns.

It is used for short-range forecasting and performs well in complex weather situations common in Central Europe.

- **Country / region:** Hungary

- **Coverage:** Hungary and surrounding Central European regions

## Notes
AROME Hungary is part of the **AROME model system**, which is used by several European national weather services for high-resolution short-range forecasting.

HungaroMet provides open access to selected forecast outputs through its national open data portal.
