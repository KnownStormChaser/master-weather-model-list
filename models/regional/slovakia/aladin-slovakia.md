# ALADIN Slovakia

## What this model is
A regional weather forecast model that predicts weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure over Slovakia and nearby regions.

It is designed for short-range forecasting and performs well in complex terrain such as mountainous areas.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : SHMÚ
- **Country / region:** Slovakia

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Slovakia and surrounding Central European regions

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional (ALADIN-based)
- **Typical resolution:** ~4–5 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~72 hours
- **Update frequency:** 4× daily

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2
- **Official download location:**  
  https://opendata.shmu.sk/meteorology/weather/nwp/aladin/

---

## Notes
ALADIN Slovakia is part of the **ALADIN limited-area modeling system**, which is used by several national weather services across Central and Eastern Europe.

SHMÚ provides open access to selected forecast outputs through its national open data portal.
