# ALADIN Slovenia

## What this model is
A regional weather forecast model that predicts weather conditions such as temperature, wind, precipitation, and pressure over Slovenia and nearby areas.

It is well suited for short-range forecasting in complex terrain.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : ARSO
- **Country / region:** Slovenia

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Slovenia and surrounding regions

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional
- **Typical resolution:** ~4 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~72 hours

## Notes
ALADIN Slovenia is part of the ALADIN limited-area model system used by several European national weather services.  
ARSO provides open access to selected forecast outputs through its national open data portal.
