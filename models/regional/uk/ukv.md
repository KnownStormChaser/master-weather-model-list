# UKV (UK Variable Resolution Model)

## What this model is
A high-resolution regional weather forecast model designed to predict small-scale and rapidly changing weather conditions such as showers, thunderstorms, fog, and local wind patterns.

It is the main short-range forecasting model used operationally in the United Kingdom.

## Notes
UKV uses variable horizontal resolution, with the highest detail over the UK.  
The openly available data provides a selected subset of variables compared to the full internal operational system.
