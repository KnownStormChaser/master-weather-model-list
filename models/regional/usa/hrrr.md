# HRRR (High-Resolution Rapid Refresh)

## What this model is
A high-resolution regional weather forecast model designed for short-range forecasting, especially for severe weather, convection, and rapidly changing conditions.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : NOAA / National Weather Service
- **Country / region:** United States

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** United States

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Regional
- **Typical resolution:** 3 km
- **Forecast length:** Up to 48 hours
- **Update frequency:** Hourly

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes
- **Data formats:** GRIB2
- **Official download location:**  
  https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/weather-climate-models/high-resolution-rapid-refresh

---

## Notes
HRRR is a convection-allowing model and is widely used for short-term forecasting of thunderstorms, fog, and wind.
