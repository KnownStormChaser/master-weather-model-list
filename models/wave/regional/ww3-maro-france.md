# WW3-MARO (Météo-France – Mediterranean)

## What this model is
WW3-MARO is a high-resolution regional wave forecast system based on **WAVEWATCH III (WW3)** tailored to the Mediterranean Sea. It forecasts wave parameters such as wave height, direction, and period.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : Météo-France
- **Country / region:** France

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Mediterranean Sea region

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Wave model (WW3)
- **Typical resolution:** ~0.10°
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~72 hours
- **Update frequency:** Operational schedule (typically 2× daily)

---

## What it predicts
- Significant wave height  
- Wave direction  
- Wave period  
- Wind wave vs swell

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes  
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes  
- **Data formats:** NetCDF

  https://meteo.data.gouv.fr/datasets/65bd19226c4e3fcbf4948f99

---

## Notes
WW3-MARO uses atmospheric forcing from the AROME weather model to provide targeted Mediterranean wave forecasts with hourly outputs.
