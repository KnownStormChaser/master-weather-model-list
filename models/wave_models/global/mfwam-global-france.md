# MFWAM (Global / Regional, Météo-France)

## What this model is
A lower-resolution version (~0.1°) of the **Météo-France WAM wave model (MFWAM)** that provides broad ocean wave forecasts. It complements higher-resolution versions by covering open ocean and extended basins.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : Météo-France
- **Country / region:** France

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Global and European ocean areas

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Wave model (MFWAM)
- **Typical resolution:** ~0.1°
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~72 hours
- **Update frequency:** Regular operational cycles

---

## What it predicts
- Significant wave height  
- Wave period  
- Wave direction  
- Wind wave vs swell components

---

## Data availability
- **Is the data free?** Yes  
- **Is the data downloadable?** Yes  
- **Data formats:** NetCDF  
- **Official download location:**  
  https://meteo.data.gouv.fr/datasets/65bd1a2957e1cc7c9625e7b5

---

## Notes
This product strikes a balance between resolution and basin-wide coverage, and is often used as a boundary or complement to higher-resolution wave forecasts.
