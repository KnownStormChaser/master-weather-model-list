# MFWAM (High-Resolution, Météo-France)

## What this model is
A high-resolution version of the **Météo-France WW3-based wave model (MFWAM)** that predicts ocean wave conditions at a very fine grid (∼0.025°). This provides detailed wave forecasts for European coastal and offshore waters.

---

## Who runs it
- **Organization:** : Météo-France
- **Country / region:** France

---

## What area it covers
- **Coverage:** Extended European ocean regions

---

## Basic details
- **Model type:** Wave model (MFWAM)
- **Typical resolution:** ~0.025°
- **Forecast length:** Up to ~72 hours
- **Update frequency:** Regular operational cycles

---

## What it predicts
- Significant wave height  
- Wave period  
- Wave direction  
- Separate swell and wind-wave components

---

The very fine resolution of this product makes it particularly useful for detailed coastal and regional marine forecasting in the European domain.
